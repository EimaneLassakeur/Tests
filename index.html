Omelette

An easy and quick dish, perfect for any meal.

Preparation time

  • Total time: 4min
  • Preparation time: 1min
  • Cooking time: 3min

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Butter
  • Salt, cumin, black peppercorns

Instructions

  • Beat the eggs
  • Heat the pan with the butter
  • Put the eggs
